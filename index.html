A Bit About Me

Ever since grade school, I've been a big math nerd. It's always been my best subject academically speaking, and I've always had a passion for training my brain logically to solve problems, whether in a puzzle or a classroom.

In college I focused on English and mathematics, and graduated with a BA in 2014. As much as I enjoyed my education, I had no idea what I would use it for professionally. After kicking the career can down the road for a few years, I started getting restless.

Throughout those years dabbling in various industries, I found myself often working on the websites of my employers: writing copy, adding content via a CMS. Eventually curiosity got the best of me and I decided to dive into the methods behind that madness.

I spent a few months working with a veteran Web Developer mentor, and over a year taking courses, reading documentation and praticing everything I could. Now I want to take that experience and apply it to your next project.
